Privacy Policy

Effective date: July 23, 2026

MyBrandFather ("MBF", "we", "us") operates mybrandfather.com. This policy explains what information we collect, why we collect it, and the choices you have. We collect as little as the work requires, and we do not sell personal information.

Information we collect

  • Contact form submissions. When you write to us through the site, we receive the name, email address, company name, and message you provide. Submissions are delivered to our inbox by Web3Forms, our form processing provider.
  • Email correspondence. If you email hello@mybrandfather.com, we retain that correspondence as part of ordinary business records.
  • Technical data. Our hosting provider, Vercel, automatically processes standard server logs (IP address, browser type, pages requested) to deliver the site securely. We do not use this data to identify you.
  • Fonts. This site loads typefaces from Google Fonts. Your browser requests those files directly from Google, which may process your IP address under Google's own privacy policy.

What we do not do

  • We do not sell, rent, or trade personal information.
  • We do not run third-party advertising or tracking pixels on this site.
  • We do not send marketing email unless you have asked for it.

How we use information

We use the information you provide to respond to inquiries, prepare proposals, deliver services, and maintain business records. The legal bases for this processing are your consent (when you contact us) and our legitimate interest in operating the business.

Retention

Inquiry correspondence is retained while it remains relevant to a current or prospective engagement, and for a reasonable period afterward for record-keeping. You may request deletion at any time.

Your rights

Depending on where you live, you may have the right to access, correct, export, or delete personal information we hold about you, and to object to certain processing. To exercise any of these rights, email hello@mybrandfather.com. We respond to verified requests within thirty days.

Third-party services

Data processors used by this site: Web3Forms (form delivery), Vercel (hosting), and Google Fonts (typefaces). Each processes data under its own privacy policy. Links on this site to external services — including our Lab products and Behance — lead to sites governed by their own policies.

Children

This site is directed at businesses and is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

Changes

If this policy changes, we will update it here with a new effective date. Material changes will be noted plainly rather than buried.
